it's nice to meet you!❤️

My name's Anny and I'm a visual designer. I graduated with a degree in Fine Arts at the University of California, Irvine. I previously worked at Gamercraft as the Lead Designer and interned at UCI Esports. Currently, I work as a freelancer taking design and video commissions!

I have always loved art and admire a lot of visuals that were done in either video games or movies/shows. It sparked my interest when I was younger to also create something impactful through imagery. Art and design have allowed me to express myself in a creative manner or simply bring an idea to life because I could! I hope to inspire others through my work as the same way I have been influenced by others.

In my free time I enjoy watching movies and playing video games. The games I play the most at the moment include Teamfight Tactics, Overwatch, & League of Legends. Other hobbies I also enjoy include baking, crotcheting, & reading!

If you'd like to contact me for inquiries or just want to chat, feel free to get in touch at contact@annytran.com
